How Instead helps reduce screen time

Instead focuses on a simple loop: measure the time already spent on a phone, connect that time to personal interests, and present concrete alternatives that feel more relevant than a generic reminder to put the device down.

1

See today’s screen time

The app can request screen time access to calculate how much time the user has spent on their phone today.

2

Choose what you want more of

Users pick interests they want to spend more time on, then update those selections later if their priorities change.

3

Get alternatives instead

Instead turns screen time into activity suggestions and lets users browse more ideas when they want another option.

Verified features from the app

These features are based on the current codebase and in-app content. Nothing below assumes a Play Store release, a backend, or unverified product behavior.

Screen time

Automatic access with a manual fallback

If screen time cannot be read automatically, the app also supports entering today’s screen time manually for the session.

Interests

Pick interests that actually matter to you

Users can choose from a wide interest catalog, including areas visible in the app like fitness, music, food and cooking, travel, art, and more.

Alternatives

Activity cards instead of vague reminders

Instead presents specific alternatives with titles, descriptions, and interest labels so users can quickly switch from scrolling to a concrete action.

Fresh ideas

More suggestions over time

The main experience supports refreshed idea timing and a “load more” flow for users who want to keep exploring additional alternatives.

Privacy

Screen time data stays on the device

The app’s permission copy explicitly states that screen time data stays on the device and is not collected, stored, or shared by the app.
